Ethical Switch is a not for profit organisation committed to helping consumers change the world through their purchases. We grew from a group of people who asked whether we could raise money for charity through our every-day spending?

Through people power we can engage with business and negotiate deals that have a sustainable and social component.

Ethical Switch campaigns have two key requirements:

  • The deals must prompt a donation by the company to our chosen charities.
  • The companies we deal with must have proven commitment to sustainability and corporate social responsibility.

Ethical Switch has emerged from a grassroots movement in Melbourne. With the help of Random Hacks of Kindness, we have launched our initial campaign to enable Victorians to switch their electricity provider and raise much needed funds for charity.
